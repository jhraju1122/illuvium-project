Voted Proposals Recap

IIP-59 Illuvium Alliance Program

Sponsor: Kieran | Illuvium.

Key Contributors: None.

Discussion: https://discord.com/channels/760344898200666112/1259683342685704293

Proposal: https://snapshot.org/#/ilv-gov.eth/proposal/0x71318143ab24de162d955fee36cfddff50b0a1997551d29a4fc3ec7bd604301c

Result: Approved.

Rationale:

  • It creates a high-quality affiliate program that encourages larger creators to promote Illuvium and offers a pathway for new creators.

  • The program is an efficient marketing strategy that aims to grow the player base by attracting new users, not just redistributing revenue from existing ones.

  • The merit-based payment system ensures only productive creators benefit, reducing financial risk for the DAO.

  • Monitoring is necessary to ensure the programs effectiveness.

  • Despite potential conflicts of interest, the program benefits the DAO and incentivizes continuous, high-quality content creation.

IIP-60 Illuvium Gameplay Feature Proposal

Sponsor: Blickter.

Key Contributors: None.

Discussion: https://discord.com/channels/760344898200666112/1257849182635425792

Proposal: https://snapshot.org/#/ilv-gov.eth/proposal/0xf70ab0e16b404874fcec3091713d07e5f160f03729f0c3f240f584177d7bf665

Result: Approved.

Rationale:

  • It clearly defines the roles of Labs, Community, and Council, ensuring each entity focuses on its strengths.

  • It addresses the previous lack of structure in the IIP process, providing clarity on what the DAO can propose and how Labs can implement it.

  • The introduction of a two-step approval process prevents wasted resources by ensuring that only feasible and community-supported ideas move forward.

  • This change simplifies the process for suggesting gameplay features, allowing Labs to act on community ideas efficiently without constant revisions or additional proposals.

  • The proposal enhances collaboration and streamlines game development.

GFP-1 Training Grounds, Illuvial Staking and TrainingDex

Sponsor: Dr.Spoon.

Key Contributors: Filow, Scoriox, MegaRatty, HappyBallz.

Discussion: https://discord.com/channels/760344898200666112/1265287128095395861

Proposal: https://snapshot.org/#/ilv-gov.eth/proposal/0x525f8d3a0152ab03a970581cd17c8d5e3089d7f8defa839152bde844eaf3ce17

Result: Approved.

Rationale:

  • Strong community engagement, refinement through multiple IIP proposals, and weeks of discussions.

  • The proposal positively impacts training, staking, and market structures, making it an additive feature.

  • Labs will evaluate, design, and integrate the concepts, ensuring a clean fit into the game.

  • This process highlights the success of GFPs, enabling community feedback in game design without the restrictions of IIPs.

  • The proposal represents a victory for decentralized governance and community-driven game development.

  • The core ideas have potential for economic integration between games and merit exploration for implementation by Labs.

IIP-61 Fuel Allocation for Marketing Purposes

Sponsor: Kieran | Illuvium

Key Contributors: None.

Discussion: https://discord.com/channels/760344898200666112/1265173455955824682

Proposal: https://snapshot.org/#/ilv-gov.eth/proposal/0xa2b9faddb6befa3a5414620fcac4b8c61ba86ff27fd4b6ff8e5956531067a528

Result: Approved.

Rationale:

  • It provides a cost-effective way to market Illuvium by giving influencers access to fuel, allowing them to create engaging content.

  • Allocating fuel directly to influencers avoids unnecessary market buybacks, conserving resources for the DAO.

  • Ensuring that fuel cannot be resold or manipulated eliminates market risks, making this a safe strategy to promote the Illuvium ecosystem.

  • This approach supports the growth of the player base, which is crucial for the game's success.

  • The allocation is not excessive, and responsible use of fuel by influencers can drive more value to the DAO.

ICCP-17 Amendment to IIP-45-R Land Revenue Share Distribution Method

Sponsor: Kieran | Illuvium.

Key Contributors: None.

Discussion: https://discord.com/channels/760344898200666112/1265601592476831787

Proposal: https://snapshot.org/#/ilv-gov.eth/proposal/0x7d25d748649a436802766228a76ecc1b2c05dea664071fe6971a69ef48b59074

Result: Approved.

Rationale:

  • It focuses on simplifying post-launch processes, which aligns with current priorities.

  • Switching from a buyback method to an ETH airdrop based on in-game activities is fairer and reduces technical complexity.

  • This change lowers the risk of market manipulation and stockpiling during buybacks, ensuring a more stable system.

  • It eliminates the issue of the DAO purchasing fuel from itself at peak prices, improving the efficiency of revenue distribution.

  • The amendment benefits the DAO and landowners by streamlining the compensation process, making it more transparent and effective.

ICCP-18 Threshold Changes to IIP-22 Revenue Distribution

Sponsor: ScrubaDubDad.

Key Contributors: Kieran | Illuvium.

Discussion: https://discord.com/channels/760344898200666112/1267722315488301086

Proposal: https://snapshot.org/#/ilv-gov.eth/proposal/0xb8207bf68175f602643ceb98ddd06989cdb33ab45d8ddefb899c9fd336036036

Result: Approved.

Rationale:

  • It has benefits in lowering the threshold for more frequent buybacks, supporting the token's price, and providing regular returns to investors.

  • It minimizes the risk of frontrunning by traders and enhances market stability through predictable and regular buybacks.

  • The original 500 ETH threshold was set under different market conditions; adjusting to a 150 ETH threshold aligns with current needs.

  • This change does not alter the fundamental mechanisms but ensures more frequent and effective revenue distributions.

IIP-63 Dynamic Survival Mode

Sponsor: !Caveman.

Key Contributors: Kieran | Illuvium.

Discussion: https://discord.com/channels/760344898200666112/1267866392057872466

Proposal: https://snapshot.org/#/ilv-gov.eth/proposal/0x39df9d43a40e6e62683565494fc95f44959332a10725b19b6de791fe06a5a4e0

Result: Declined.

Rationale:

  • Labs is already developing a dynamic survival mode to fix the multi-accounting exploit. It is counterproductive to propose changes for a feature that is already in progress. This proposal might delay the solution, which is time-sensitive.

  • The complete removal of survival wave points is too extreme. While addressing exploiters is necessary, wiping points retroactively could cause more harm than a balanced adjustment to point values.

  • Preferred Solution: Support ICCP-XX Airdrop Points Revision for a more equitable approach. Exploit fixes should remain under Labs' purview, and retroactive changes should be carefully reconsidered.

Add Cooldown to Overworld Runs - Anti Cheat

Author: Web3GamingTv.

Upvotes: 26.

Downvotes: 0.

Objective:

  • Introduce a 3-10 minute cooldown between starting a run and traveling again to reduce the effectiveness of auto-scanning and teleporting cheats. This cooldown will not impact normal players but will make cheats less advantageous, reducing the need for an auto-scan feature.

  • Additionally, it hinders multi-account exploitation by lowering efficiency.
